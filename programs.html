Our Programs

Comprehensive medical education programs designed to inspire and educate the next generation

Program Information

How to Enroll

All programs are free and open to students in the specified age ranges. Simply click on the program you're interested in to learn more and sign up.

Schedule

Programs run on a seasonal basis with new cohorts starting every few months. Check individual program pages for specific dates and times.

Format

We offer both online and in-person sessions depending on the program and location. All materials and resources are provided free of charge.

Completion

Students who complete a program receive a certificate of completion and are eligible to enroll in advanced programs where available.

Available Programs

Explore our carefully designed educational programs that make medical learning accessible and engaging for young minds.

Human Body Fundamentals

8-Week Program

Available Now

This eight-week program helps children explore the essential systems of the human body through interactive lessons. Students will gain foundational knowledge of major body systems, medical terminology, vital organs, sensory perception, health practices, and key medical tools and professions.

Ages 8-14 (3rd-8th Grade)
8 Weekly Sessions
Completely Free
No Prerequisites

What You'll Learn:

Body Systems Overview
Circulation & Respiration
Nutrition & Digestion
Movement & Support
Brain & Communication
Growth & Development
Immune Response
Health & Wellness
