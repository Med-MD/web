Human Body Fundamentals

An 8-week interactive journey through the amazing systems of the human body

Human Body Fundamentals
This eight-week program helps children explore the essential systems of the human body through interactive lessons. Students will gain foundational knowledge of major body systems, medical terminology, vital organs, sensory perception, health practices, and key medical tools and professions.
Age Range
8 - 14 years
Grade Levels
3rd - 8th Grade
Prerequisites
None Required
Cost
Free
Week 1
Intro to the Human Body
Overview of body systems, basic anatomy, and introduction to medical terminology.
Week 2
Circulation and Respiration
Explore the circulatory and respiratory systems, blood flow, and breathing mechanisms.
Week 3
Nutrition and Digestion
Understanding nutrition, the digestive system, and how the body processes food.
Week 4
Movement and Support
Study the musculoskeletal system, bones, muscles, tissues, joints, and how movement occurs.
Week 5
The Brain and Communication
Discover the nervous system, the brain, and how the body communicates actions.
Week 6
Growth, Development, and Emotions
Learn about human development, the endocrine system, and hormones.
Week 7
The Immune Response
Understand the integumentary system and how different white blood cells protect against disease.
Week 8
Health and Wellness
Explore homeostasis, best health practices, and different medical professions.
