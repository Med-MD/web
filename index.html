Why Was MedMD Created?

"At MedMD, our mission is to cultivate a passion for medicine and healthcare by providing engaging, student-led educational experiences for young learners. We believe that early exposure to the medical field can inspire curiosity, build knowledge, and open doors to many future opportunities.

Our organization is aimed to close the gap between aspiring high school students interested in medicine and K-8 students eager to learn. Through interactive lessons and hands-on activities, we aim to make medical education accessible, exciting, and impactful. By nurturing a supportive learning environment, we encourage young minds to explore the vast world of healthcare, empowering them with knowledge and confidence to pursue their future interests.

MedMD is more than just an educational programit is a community of students dedicated to making a difference. We work to spark enthusiasm for medicine, foster future leaders in healthcare, and create a lasting impact on students of all ages. Through collaboration, innovation, and a shared passion for learning, we are shaping the future of medicine, one lesson at a time."

- Katherine Xu, Co-Founder & Teacher

Programs

Human Body Fundamentals
This eight-week program helps children explore the essential systems of the human body through interactive lessons. Students will gain foundational knowledge of major body systems, medical terminology, vital organs, sensory perception, health practices, and key medical tools and professions.
Age Range
8 - 14 years
Grade Levels
3rd - 8th Grade
Prerequisites
None Required
Cost
Free
Week 1
Intro to the Human Body
Overview of body systems, basic anatomy, and introduction to medical terminology.
Week 2
Circulation and Respiration
Explore the circulatory and respiratory systems, blood flow, and breathing mechanisms.
Week 3
Nutrition and Digestion
Understanding nutrition, the digestive system, and how the body processes food.
Week 4
Movement and Support
Study the musculoskeletal system, bones, muscles, tissues, joints, and how movement occurs.
Week 5
The Brain and Communication
Discover the central/peripheral nervous system, the brain, and how the body communicates actions.
Week 6
Growth, Development, and Emotions
Learn about human development, the endocrine system, and hormones.
Week 7
The Immune Response
Understand the integumentary system and how different white blood cells protect against disease.
Week 8
Health and Wellness
Explore homeostasis, best health practices, and different medical professions.
